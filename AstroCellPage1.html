Road Rules Prototype

Summary

This project was my capstone made of a five man team. Astro-Cell, is a multiplayer puzzle survival game where you play as a prisoner and guards in an intergalactic prison. You and your fellow cell mates must escape your cells and sneak around the prison working towards escaping. You must complete tasks and find items that will aid in your escape while avoiding prison guards and the Warden. If a Guard finds you with contraband you are returned to your cell and must wait to be released by a fellow cellmate or after your confinement time is up. If a guard searches someone with no contraband then they are forced to go update their artificial intelligence.

Role

During this project I was tasked with creating and implimenting the item system for this game. As a player you can go to chests scattered about the prison and open the inventory and either grab or place items depending on the type of chest. there are multiple types of stashes eaech with their own purpose. first being a generic stash which have the items you can find throughout the map. players cannot place items into these chests. The second being a hidden stash. This stash can be used to place and retrieve items from, mainly used for hiding items from guards or trading to other players. The third type of chest is a crafting stash which will take items from a player and they can select the type of item they would like to make. If they have the right components they will be rewarded with the crafted item and the crafting components will be sent back to their starting chest. Additionally there are chests that will provide guards with items used to fix sabotages. All of which work over a network and reflect over the server.

In addition to the items system I implimented the guard search mechanic in which guards can search a player for contraband. When a search is executed both players are locked in place and the guard will see the players inventory but no display items and names. the guard must then guess what item might be contraband. If correct the prisoner will be sent to a cell and their items will be returned to their repective chests. If the guard guesses wrong the prisoner is able to run away and the guards movement is locked as they must wait out a timer

Features

  • Online Multiplayer Game
  • Play as a team to escape from your space prison or cooridinate between your fellow guards and stop the prisoners from escaping
  • Sabotage systems to make escape easier
  • craft items used to sabotage or hold down guards
  • move items around the prison in hidden stashes and itme vents
