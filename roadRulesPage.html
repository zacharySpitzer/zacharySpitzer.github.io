Road Rules Prototype

Features

  • informative learning tool designed to make instruction of road saftey and rules fun and interactive.
  • Carefully examine traffic scenarios to determine Who is following Regulations.
  • Pass around your device to others to see if they can answer the questions.

Purpose

This project was designed to demonstate what a fun and interactive Road Rules learning experience could look like. It's simple in nature and effective in its ability to teach something an image cant always show in an effecient way. reading from a book can at times be very challenging for some, as not everyone learns the same way. so by providing students with a new point of view and a more interactable experience students could be better aided in their learning.
